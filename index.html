About

Created by Rob Sim because he needed to create icons for iOS 7 in a simple way. Edit once, have all the icons you need come out in a folder. Includes iTunesArtwork, iTunesArtwork@2x, iPhone, iPhone@2x, iPad, iPad@2x, Settings, Settings@2x, Spotlight and Spotlight@2x. All the icons you need for production, ready to go!

These are free to use, but please do not distribute them! I'd love it if you linked back here if you use them anywhere! Enjoy!
